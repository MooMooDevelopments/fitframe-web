Built responsibly

Non-negotiable
safety rules.

No weight or BMI

We never ask for, display, or track weight, BMI, or calorie data anywhere in the app. Not optional — not configurable.

Relative scoring only

Your progress score reflects your own history — not a comparison to other bodies. Leaderboards show score, not photos.

Private by default

Every upload is stored privately. Public sharing requires an explicit opt-in. Revocable at any time in settings.

Content moderation

Public posts can be reported with one tap. Reason picker covers inappropriate, fake, or spam. Reports go to a review queue.
